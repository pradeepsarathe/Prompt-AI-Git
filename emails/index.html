Newsletter system

How PromptAI email works

Two emails power the whole program: a Welcome that fires the instant someone subscribes, and a Weekly Digest that's auto-built from the live feed and sent every Tuesday. Both are below — exactly as subscribers will see them.

The flow

From the subscribe box on the homepage to an inbox — fully automated.

1

Subscribe

Visitor enters their email on the site. It's saved server-side in SUBSCRIBERS KV — synced, not per-device.

2

Welcome email

Instantly, /subscribe sends the welcome email through Resend. Sets expectations and links back to the site.

3

Weekly build

Every Tuesday a scheduled call to /send-digest pulls the top HN story + arXiv papers + headlines live.

4

Send to all

The digest is rendered per-subscriber (own unsubscribe link) and sent in batches to the whole list.

To go fully live

The code is wired and deployed. These are the account-level steps only you can do — about 15 minutes.

1
Create a Resend account (free up to 3,000 emails/mo) at resend.com and verify your sending domain so mail comes from hello@promptai.in.
2
Add the secrets in Cloudflare Pages → Settings → Functions: RESEND_API_KEY, FROM_EMAIL (e.g. PromptAI <hello@promptai.in>), and a random CRON_SECRET.
3
Add the KV namespaces — bindings named SUBSCRIBERS (already used) and USERS (for cross-device login).
4
Schedule the weekly send — point a free scheduler (cron-job.org) or a Cloudflare Worker cron at /send-digest?key=CRON_SECRET, Tuesdays at 8am.

Until Resend keys are added, everything still works gracefully: subscriptions are saved, the site runs, and login falls back to local mode in preview. Nothing breaks — emails just don't send yet.
